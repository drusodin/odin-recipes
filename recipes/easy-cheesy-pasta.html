Easy Cheesy Pasta

Description

This is one of my go-to pasta recipes. It's quick, easy to make, and tastes absolutely fire. There's only a few ingredients, stuff many Americans would have laying around. I recommend going for a quality cheese, but no shame if all you have is that powdery Kraft stuff in the shaker bottle. It will get the job done!

Ingredients

    90 g Dried pasta (Spaghetti recommended)
    1/2 Cup Whole Milk or Heavy Whipping Cream
    As much Parmesan cheese as your heart desires
    14 g (1 tbsp) Salted Butter
    Salt and pepper
